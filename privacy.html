We are the data controller for the personal data you provide when subscribing to actually.matters.

What data we collect

We collect as little as possible:

  • Email address — required to send you the newsletter.
  • Name — optional, only if you provide it.

We do not use tracking pixels. We do not build advertising profiles. We do not sell your data.

Why we collect it

The legal basis is consent under Article 6(1)(a) of the GDPR. You subscribed; we send you the newsletter. That is the full scope of how we use your data.

You can withdraw consent at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email we send.

Who processes your data

We use Buttondown (buttondown.com) to manage subscriptions and send emails. Buttondown is based in the United States and acts as our data processor.

Data transfers to the US are covered under applicable Standard Contractual Clauses. You can read Buttondown's privacy policy at buttondown.com/legal/privacy.

No other third parties have access to your personal data.

How long we keep it

We keep your email address for as long as you are subscribed. When you unsubscribe, your data is deleted within 30 days.

Your rights under GDPR

As an EU resident you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Erasure — ask us to delete your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw consent — unsubscribe at any time via any email we send.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@actuallymatters.co. We will respond within 30 days.

Complaints

If you believe we are not handling your data correctly, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Dutch Data Protection Authority:

autoriteitpersoonsgegevens.nl

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will notify subscribers by email before the changes take effect. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the current version.
